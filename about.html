About This Project

Timeline of Consoles is an interactive timeline of home gaming systems. The timeline spans from 1972 (Magnavox Odyssey) to 2020 (PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X). It features technical specifications, iconic games, and key milestones in the development of each console. All data has been verified to be historically accurate.
