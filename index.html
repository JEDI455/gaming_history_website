1970s - 1980s

The Arcade Golden Age

Coin-operated entertainment dominated the landscape. Classics like Pong, Pac-Man, and Space Invaders defined a generation of early electronic gaming.

1980s - 1990s

The Rise of Consoles

Gaming moved into the living room. The 8-bit era brought immersive, narrative-driven experiences, revolutionizing how stories were told through gameplay.

Mid 1990s

The 3D Revolution

Polygons replaced sprites. The leap to 3D unlocked vast exploring worlds, giving birth to genres that are still beloved and played today.

2000s - Present

Modern Realism

Photorealistic graphics, massive multiplayer worlds, and esports transformed gaming into the largest entertainment industry on the globe.
